Flu Vaccinations at Trent Pharmacy

Every year around September, we offer both NHS and private (paid) flu vaccinations at Trent Pharmacy.

Book Your NHS and Private Flu Jab in Burton-on-Trent

Who is eligible for the NHS Flu Service?

Anyone over 18 years old can book a standard flu jab (subject to consultation). To qualify for an NHS flu vaccination, you must meet one of the following criteria:

  • Aged over 65 years old
  • Pregnant
  • A carer

Or you must have certain medical conditions, including:

  • Asthma and COPD
  • Heart disease
  • Kidney disease
  • Liver disease
  • Neurological disease
  • Diabetes
