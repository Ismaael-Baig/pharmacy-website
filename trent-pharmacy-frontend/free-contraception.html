Can I get Free oral contraception without a prescription?

At Trent Pharmacy, we offer the NHS contraception service. Our pharmacist is able to start women on oral contraception (also known as ‘the Pill’) for the first time or restart them after a break, without the need to see a GP.

This is an extension to the existing contraception service that allows women to visit our pharmacy for repeat supply of contraception that was initially prescribed by a GP.

The pharmacist will provide a confidential consultation to discuss your current medication and ensure it is the best option for your needs.

Oral contraception is free on the NHS, and you will not be charged any prescription fees.

Free Emergency Hormonal Contraception

Trent Pharmacy also offers the NHS-commissioned free emergency hormonal contraception, also known as the ‘morning after pill’ or ‘Plan B’ in Burton-on-Trent.

Please walk in for a consultation or call 01283 564928 to book an appointment with the pharmacist.
