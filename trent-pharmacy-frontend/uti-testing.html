UTI Testing & Treatment in Burton-on-Trent

For Women aged 16-64

You can walk in or contact our pharmacy team. Alternatively, your GP surgery or NHS 111 may refer you electronically for a consultation under this service, giving you faster access to healthcare advice and treatment.

All consultations are private, and any advice or treatment will be sent to your GP.
